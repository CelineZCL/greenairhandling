Our Story

Committed to Excellence

Founded in 2019, Greenair Handling has quickly grown from a small regional supplier to a leading international provider of HVAC ventilation products. Our journey has been driven by a commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Today, we serve HVAC distributors, installers, and contractors in over 50 countries across Europe, North America, and beyond. Our comprehensive product range includes air valves, diffusers, louvers, dampers, duct accessories, and custom HVAC fittings.

What sets us apart is our dedication to building long-term partnerships with our customers. We don't just sell products – we provide solutions, technical support, and the reliability that our partners need to succeed in their markets.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality HVAC ventilation products that enable our customers to create comfortable, healthy, and energy-efficient indoor environments. We achieve this through continuous innovation, strict quality control, and exceptional customer service.

Our Vision

To be the world's most trusted supplier of HVAC ventilation products, recognized for our quality, reliability, and commitment to sustainable building practices. We aim to contribute to healthier indoor environments globally.

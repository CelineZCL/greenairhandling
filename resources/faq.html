Products

What materials are your products made from?

Our standard products are manufactured from high-quality galvanized steel. We also offer options in aluminum, stainless steel (304 and 316), and powder-coated finishes for specific applications. Contact us for material specifications for your project requirements.

Can I order custom sizes?

Yes, we offer custom manufacturing for most of our product lines. Custom sizes, materials, and configurations are available with minimum order quantities. Contact our sales team with your specifications for a quote.

Do your products meet international standards?

Yes, our products are manufactured to meet or exceed relevant international standards including EN, ISO, and UL standards where applicable. Fire dampers are tested and certified to EN 1366-2 and UL 555 standards. Certificates are available upon request.

What is the warranty on your products?

All our products come with a standard 2-year warranty against manufacturing defects. Extended warranties are available for specific product lines. Please refer to our warranty documentation for full terms and conditions.

Ordering & Shipping

What is the minimum order quantity?

For standard products, we typically have a minimum order value of €500. For custom products, minimum order quantities vary by product type. Contact our sales team for specific MOQ information.

What are your lead times?

Standard products typically ship within 2-3 weeks from order confirmation. Custom products have lead times of 4-6 weeks depending on complexity and order volume. Rush orders may be accommodated - please contact us to discuss.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide. We work with major freight forwarders to ensure reliable delivery to any destination. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Contact us for a shipping quote.

What payment terms do you offer?

For new customers, we require payment in advance or letter of credit. For established customers with approved credit, we offer net 30 payment terms. Contact our accounting department to discuss payment options.

Technical

How do I select the right product for my application?

Our product pages include selection guides and technical specifications. For complex projects, our technical team can assist with product selection based on your airflow requirements, pressure ratings, and installation constraints. Contact us for assistance.

Do you provide CAD drawings?

Yes, CAD drawings (DWG and PDF formats) are available for all our products. These can be downloaded from individual product pages or requested from our technical support team.

Can I get product samples?

Yes, we provide samples for evaluation purposes. Sample requests are subject to approval and may incur shipping costs. Contact our sales team to request samples.

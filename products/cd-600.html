CD-600

Ceiling Diffuser 600x600

The CD-600 ceiling diffuser is designed for optimal air distribution in commercial and residential spaces. Its 4-way blow pattern ensures even airflow throughout the room, while the removable core allows for easy cleaning and maintenance access.

Manufactured from high-quality aluminum, this diffuser is lightweight yet durable. The standard 600x600mm face size fits standard ceiling grids, making installation quick and straightforward. The powder-coated white finish blends seamlessly with most ceiling designs.

Key Features

  • 4-way airflow pattern for even distribution
  • Removable core for easy maintenance
  • Aluminum construction - lightweight and durable
  • Standard 600x600mm face size
  • Powder-coated white finish
  • Compatible with standard ceiling grids
  • Low noise operation

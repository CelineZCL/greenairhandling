Acoustic Louvers Range

Sound attenuation solutions for noise-sensitive applications.

Greenair Handling is pleased to announce the expansion of our acoustic louver product line, offering enhanced noise reduction capabilities for sensitive applications.

New Product Range

Our expanded acoustic louver series includes models with noise reduction ratings from 10dB to 35dB, providing solutions for a wide range of applications from light commercial to heavy industrial noise control.

Key Features

Advanced Acoustic Media: Our louvers feature high-performance sound-absorbing materials that maintain effectiveness over the product lifetime, even in challenging environmental conditions.

Optimized Airflow: Engineered blade profiles minimize pressure drop while maximizing acoustic performance, ensuring ventilation systems operate efficiently.

Durable Construction: Available in aluminum, galvanized steel, and stainless steel options to suit various environmental requirements and aesthetic preferences.

Applications

The new range is ideal for data centers, hospitals, residential buildings near highways, recording studios, and any application where external noise intrusion must be minimized while maintaining proper ventilation.

Technical Support

Our engineering team provides acoustic modeling and product selection assistance to ensure optimal performance for your specific project requirements. Contact us for detailed specifications and application guidance.

Availability

The expanded acoustic louver range is now available for order with standard lead times. Custom sizes and finishes can be accommodated for large projects.

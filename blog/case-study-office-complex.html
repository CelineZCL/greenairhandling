Case Study: Office Complex

How we helped a major office complex improve their ventilation system.

How Greenair Handling helped a major office development achieve optimal air quality and energy efficiency through a comprehensive HVAC ventilation solution.

Project Overview

A new 25-story office complex in a major European city required a complete HVAC ventilation system for its 500,000 square feet of premium office space. The project demanded high-performance products that could meet strict energy efficiency targets while providing superior indoor air quality.

The Challenge

The building's design called for a flexible HVAC system capable of serving diverse tenant requirements. Key challenges included varying occupancy patterns across floors, the need for zone-level temperature control, and compliance with local energy codes requiring heat recovery on all ventilation systems.

Our Solution

Greenair Handling supplied a complete range of ventilation products including:

• Linear Diffusers: Sleek architectural diffusers for open ceiling areas

• Volume Control Dampers: Motorized dampers for precise zone control

• Weather Louvers: High-performance intake and exhaust louvers

• Fire Dampers: UL-rated dampers for life safety compliance

• Acoustic Louvers: Sound-attenuating louvers for noise-sensitive areas

Results

The completed system exceeded performance expectations, achieving 35% better energy efficiency than code requirements. Tenants report excellent comfort levels, and the building has received LEED Gold certification. The flexible design allows for easy reconfiguration as tenant needs evolve.

Client Testimonial

"Greenair Handling's products and technical support were instrumental in the success of this project. Their team's expertise helped us navigate complex design challenges, and the quality of their ventilation components is exceptional." — Project Director

Motorized Damper Series

Exploring our complete range of motorized dampers for HVAC automation.

Introducing our latest range of motorized dampers featuring integrated smart controls for modern building management systems.

Smart Control Integration

Our new motorized damper series features native BACnet and Modbus communication, enabling seamless integration with building automation systems. Real-time position feedback, airflow measurement, and diagnostic data are available through standard protocols.

Advanced Features

Precise Positioning: High-resolution actuators provide accurate airflow control with position repeatability within 0.5 degrees.

Self-Diagnostics: Built-in intelligence monitors actuator health and blade operation, alerting facility managers to maintenance needs before failures occur.

Energy Harvesting: Optional Belimo ZIP economy function captures energy from airflow to power the actuator, eliminating the need for external power in certain applications.

Product Range

The series includes rectangular dampers from 4" x 4" to 60" x 60" and round dampers from 4" to 36" diameter. All models are available with spring-return or non-spring-return actuators and various control signal options.

Applications

These smart dampers are ideal for VAV systems, demand-controlled ventilation, zone control, and any application requiring precise airflow management with building automation integration.
